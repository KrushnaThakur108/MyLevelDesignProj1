Game Overview: hocus.

hocus.—stylized with a trailing dot—is a captivating puzzle experience that blends minimalist design with mind-bending optical illusions inspired by the art of M.C. Escher. Its premise is simple yet profound: guide a red cube through a maze of impossible shapes, floating platforms, and paradoxical paths.
